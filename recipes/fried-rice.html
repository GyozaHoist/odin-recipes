Fried Rice

Hello eveyone! I still wanna share Indonesian recipes.

Still made from rice. Special Javanese Fried Rice (Nasi Goreng Jawa)

Ingredients:

  • 1 plate of white rice
  • Sliced beef stew (100 g)
  • Cleaned & peeled shrimp (50 g)
  • Sweet soy sauce (1 tablespoon)
  • Boiled water (1 tablespoon)
  • Oil (2 tabllespoon)
Seasoning:
  • Onion (3 cloves)
  • Red pepper(2 pieces)
  • Garlic (1/2 cloves)
  • Coriander (1/2 teaspoon_)
  • Salt (1/2 teaspoon)
  • Sugar (1/2 teaspoon)
  • Shrimp paste (1/2 teaspoon)
Additionals:
  • Fried egg
  • Fried onion
  • Prawn crackers
  • Celery leaves
  • Cucumber slices
  • Tomato slices

How to make:

  1. First, puree the seasoning of salt, shrimp paste, sugar and coriander.
  2. After it's smooth, add garlic, red chilli, red onion, and puree back.
  3. Heat the cooking oil on the frying pan and then add the pureed seasoning
  4. After the seasoning smells sweet, add sweet soy sauce with water, stir it evenly until foaming.
  5. Put shrimp and slices of beef, then stir until shrimp change color.
  6. Last, put the white rice, stir it until the spices and rice evenly and pervasive. Do it with a little emphasis so that the rice does not clot
  7. Afte it looks good and stirred evenly, turn off the fire and served them on a plate.
  8. To increase the taste, serve Javanese Fried Rice with additional ingredients customized to your taste. Enjoy!
